Company overview

Alishia is a company registered as a private limited company registered under the Companies Act 2013. aLishia is also registered under the government of India as MSME (Small & Medium enterprise).

A sister brother duo, post a successful career in Human Resource Management and Hospitality respectively, thought of a venture together – that is how aLishia came into existence. A total of over 20 years of experience is what aLishia has

Alishia Talent Management is a forerunner in designing Human Resources solutions (Talent Acquisition, HR Shared Services, HR Processes Automation Services) specific to the needs of our respected clients.

Alishia’s second business model, “Saarthi” caters to individuals for their specific career & counselling needs.

We follow a strict Code of Conduct and the difference lies in our capability to understand multifarious requirements, quick delivery and 24X7 accessibility. A team of experienced consultants’ expertise at all levels and operating in 7 different industry verticals, Alishia Talent Management meets the requirements of any corporation, large or small, already acting PAN India with an ambitious to expand internationally.

Our service includes a comprehensive consultation to help identify gaps and opportunities, a comprehensive solution that includes a project plan with timelines and milestones, a cost analysis, and a schedule. We also offer a suite of quality products that will help you get there quickly and smoothly. That’s how we ensure your success.

Alishia’s approach to clients is that of partnership, we don’t make clients we build partnerships
